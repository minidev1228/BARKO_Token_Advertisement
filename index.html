    ABOUT

    Barko is a groundbreaking cryptocurrency project dedicated to promoting dog welfare while empowering the crypto community with innovative features. As the king of crypto, Barko represents leadership, positivity, and growth, with a unique mission that blends financial empowerment and charitable giving.

    With a focus on supporting animal welfare, 1% of every transaction goes directly to charity, ensuring that our impact extends beyond the digital world to help real-world causes. Another 1% is allocated to the ecosystem fund, which fuels community rewards, ongoing development, and future enhancements. Barko offers community rewards of up to $10,000, giving holders the chance to be rewarded for their participation and support.

    With a presale divided into five stages, early investors have a unique opportunity to join a growing community and support creative projects through our dog-themed NFT marketplace.

    For NFT holders, there’s an even greater opportunity: a chance to win rewards ranging from $5,000 to $10,000. Even without holding an NFT, participants can still earn rewards ranging from $1 to $5,000, ensuring everyone in the community has a chance to benefit.

    Barko isn’t just a token – it’s a vibrant ecosystem. We aim to create exciting opportunities through NFTs, event-based games, and a thriving community. Barko holders will also enjoy the benefits of a deflationary model, community voting mechanisms, and interactive features like contests and games.

    Join us as we lead the charge toward a better future for both crypto enthusiasts and our four-legged friends.
